BUSINESS DICTIONARY
100+ terms.
CAC
Customer Acquisition Cost — how much it costs to acquire one paying customer through all marketing and sales spend.
PMF
Product-Market Fit — the moment your product genuinely satisfies a specific market demand at scale.
Runway
How many months your company can operate before running out of cash at the current burn rate.
Term Sheet
A non-binding agreement outlining the key terms of an investment deal before legal documentation.
Burn Rate
The rate at which a company spends its cash reserves. Monthly burn = money out minus money in.
Dilution
The reduction in existing shareholders' ownership percentage when new shares are issued in a funding round.

The Founder

Mohammad Shahan Khan

Founder · Builder · Product Thinker

FounderPath started with a simple frustration: most entrepreneurship education focuses on theory, not reality.

Building a company is not a sequence of lessons. It is a chain of decisions made under uncertainty, pressure, and incomplete information.

I wanted to design something that reflects that reality. FounderPath is my attempt to turn entrepreneurship into a lived experience rather than passive learning — a place where you learn to think like a founder before you ever take real-world risks.
