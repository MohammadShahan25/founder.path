BUSINESS DICTIONARY
100+ terms. No MBA required.
CAC
Customer Acquisition Cost — how much it costs to acquire one paying customer through all marketing and sales spend.
PMF
Product-Market Fit — the moment your product genuinely satisfies a specific market demand at scale.
Runway
How many months your company can operate before running out of cash at the current burn rate.
Term Sheet
A non-binding agreement outlining the key terms of an investment deal before legal documentation.
Burn Rate
The rate at which a company spends its cash reserves. Monthly burn = money out minus money in.
Dilution
The reduction in existing shareholders' ownership percentage when new shares are issued in a funding round.
